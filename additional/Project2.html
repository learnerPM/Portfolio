Description

Hospital Finder is an app-based solution for the problems that common people face during the time of medical emergencies, people always face the problem in deciding which hospital they should visit based on their medical emergencies. This app will provide a list of hospitals based on available medical treatment, specialist doctors, medicine/blood availability, etc.
